I’m a third-year PhD student at Ghent University in Cap-lab (PI: Gilles Pourtois; https://www.cap-lab.be/).

I’m interested in neurocognitive mechanisms of favorable influences of negative emotion on cognitive control, and how does it change with further regulation (via reappraisal strategy) of negative emotion.

During my master period, my research topic mainly focused on emotional conflict. I investigated how the processing priority of negative emotion improves emotional conflict control, as well as how emotional conflict was modulated by perceptual load.
